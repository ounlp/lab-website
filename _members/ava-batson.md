Ava Batson-Perez is an undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science at the University of Oklahoma. She is an Undergraduate Research Assistant in the OU Natural Language Processing (OUNLP) Lab, Native Outreach Chair of the American Indian Science and Engineering Society (AISES), and a former Software Engineering Intern at Google. Her research focuses on creating intelligent agents powered by large language models that can work together and act independently in virtual environments such as Minecraft, using tools and feedback from their surroundings to solve tasks.
