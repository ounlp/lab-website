Research

What we study

Building robust, interpretable AI systems that support human learning, communication, and collaboration.

Dialogue & Discourse

Multi-party and multi-modal dialogue systems and discourse-aware modeling.

Structured Prediction

Discourse-aware representation learning and structured prediction over language.

LLM Alignment & Agents

Large-language-model alignment and agentic systems that act and collaborate.

Multimodal Interaction

Grounded, multi-modal communication across speech, vision, and text.

Retrieval-Augmented Generation

Retrieval-augmented and grounded generative models for reliable answers.

Trustworthy AI

Reliable, interpretable AI for education and healthcare contexts.

Our work

Reliable human–AI language systems

From multi-party dialogue and LLM agents to structured prediction and trustworthy deployment, our projects span the path from research models to real-world use in education and healthcare.
